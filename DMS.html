Delivery Management System (DMS)

https://dms.bjshomedelivery.com/

The Delivery Management System (DMS) was designed to streamline end-to-end delivery operations, improve real-time visibility, and reduce manual coordination between teams.

The Delivery Management System (DMS) was redesigned to improve operational efficiency, real-time delivery visibility, and coordination between warehouse teams, delivery executives, and operations managers.

The platform served a diverse user base aged 20 to 60+, requiring a highly intuitive interface with minimal learning curve.

Through stakeholder interviews, workflow mapping sessions, and operational analysis, I identified key friction points in delivery tracking, task allocation, and communication flow.

The redesign focused on simplifying complex logistics workflows into a structured, dashboard-driven system that improved clarity, usability, and scalability.

  • Led UX strategy and end-to-end workflow redesign
  • Conducted stakeholder interviews and requirement analysis
  • Created user flows, wireframes, and high-fidelity prototypes in Figma
  • Designed role-based dashboards for multiple user types
  • Collaborated with product managers and engineering teams during implementation
  • Ensured usability across diverse age groups (20–60+ users)
